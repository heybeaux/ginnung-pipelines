## Caption: Adam vs Rainman sparring

Adam thought it was funny to tell us all stories about ghost, so we were all very excited to see him go all out sparring with "Rainman". Rainman has been training for close to a year and is preparing himself for a competition. Adam has been training for 3 weeks and his fight résumé lists: 2 x pub fight, 1 x sparring session. Needless to say we thought this fight was a bit unevenly matched. That is until Adam showed us what he had hidden up his sleeve… This guy has an amazing reach. He actually had more reach in his punches than Rainman had in his kicks. He listened to the advice we have him before the match about getting in close to eliminate Rainman's kicking ability, and whenever he felt overwhelmed he just needed to take Rainman's legs out. Adam was graceful and moved well throughout the entire fight. Even as he became fatigued he knew when to step in and when to withdraw. He also possesses that fighter's spirit. He was amazing to watch. Good work Adam. Next time try not to smile so much after kicking the guy's arse. Ps. The blood on Adam's shirt belongs to Rainman. It's not from Adam's nose. Nice!

## Caption: Market street drivers

This is the view of the markets along the street. Unlike Australia, nobody sees the point in blocking off the road for traffic. As a result, the day is a constant game of "dodge the crazy Chinese driver on a scooter or in a car". The driver is always kind enough to sit on the horn for the entire duration of their drive down market street, so you know when they're behind you. The sights and smells were amazing. It was such a full on experience. The best part was probably the fact that everyone stopped to stare at us because we were westerners. Kids and women would often run up to say "Hello" and then run off.
