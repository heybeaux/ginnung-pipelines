# Haunted House

My friend Adam from the UK told us all a wonderful story. Unfortunately it involved seeing the ghost of a little girl in the hallway at 3am. We all laughed it off. However now that he has planted that seed, we're all seeing things that aren't there.

Damn you Adam.
