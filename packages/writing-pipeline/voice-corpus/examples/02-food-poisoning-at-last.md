# Food poisoning at last

During my travels, I've made a point of eating at some of the dodgiest places I can find. Part of me hopes I'll find some hidden gem that others will overlook. Another strange, twisted part of me just wants to experience food poisoning.

No hidden gems as yet. And I can safely say… food poisoning sucks.
