# Basics = Check

Today I was told by my Sifu that I have passed the basics of Wing Chun. The basic steps, stances and punches have now been sufficiently learned and I can move on to one of the 11 forms of Wing Chun. It's a good feeling to know that I'm actually making progress. I just need to keep in mind that passed by no means means mastered. These steps must be practised daily.

The basics took 7 days of lessons. I hear the first form takes two months. I'm in for a long journey.
