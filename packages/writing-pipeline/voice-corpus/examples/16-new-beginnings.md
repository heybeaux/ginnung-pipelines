# New beginnings

Dear Beaux,

Welcome to January 27th. Today is a new day. It is the beginning of a new journey. There are no safety nets. There are no second chances. There is no one to hold your hand. Every lesson you've learnt has prepared you for where you are now. Stand tall and be proud of who you are. You've got what it takes.

Sometimes things are going to get super rough. Just remember to stand back, regroup and breathe. Realise that sometimes you just need to "let go".

We are where we want to be.

Love,
Beaux.
