# Return to civilisation

In just under two weeks I'll be boarding a plane headed for Australia. I've been a little bit excited about this trip ever since I arrived in China, however now that it's only days away, I'm not so sure.

The last two months have been a challenge. Mentally, physically and emotionally my boundaries have been pushed. Now I feel like I'm headed back to… Easy Town. I'm not going to have someone telling me to run faster, jump higher or correct my kicking technique. I'm going to be getting lattes and eating whatever I want. I'm going to be sleeping in. I'm going to be free of the constant pain I feel in my muscles. I think that scares me a little.

Who would have thought that this "crap" lifestyle I've had the last couple of months would become so comfortable? I enjoy being pushed. I enjoy being broken down and then being built back up.

Isn't it funny the way things turn out?
