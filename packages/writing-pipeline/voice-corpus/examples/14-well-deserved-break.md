# Well deserved break

I've been pretty quiet lately as I have spent the last week or so back in Australia. No martial arts, no masters hitting me or yelling "more power", no stair runs, no injuries. It has still been pretty hard physically as I've been trying to maintain my fitness and flexibility. Every time I think of it I stop to punch a tree or a wall to help my conditioning. I've been training with a close friend who is prepping for Special Forces selection next year. I'm staying focused.

The strange thing about my trip home is how many people hang on my every word about what I'm doing. People listen intently, then ask questions as fast as possible to get as much information as they can to paint a mental picture of what I'm doing. They all say the same thing, "that's incredible, I wish I was younger, I wish I was doing that." I almost feel like a celebrity now that I'm home. But the weird thing is when I go back to China next week, I'll be surrounded by people doing the same thing as myself. I'll stop being so interesting and go back to just being another student.

I'm honestly not sure which life I appreciate more.
