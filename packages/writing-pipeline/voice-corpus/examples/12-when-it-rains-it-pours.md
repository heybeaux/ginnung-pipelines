# When it rains, it pours

Today I will be doing my first lot of form gradings since being in China. I've worked on some of these forms for as long as I've been in China (about 3 months now), and others, nowhere near as long. Usually a student will grade as they go. They will be assessed on their basics, and a month or so later their first form, and so on. Today however, I will be grading 3 forms. I will have my basics and first form assessed, followed by the sparring form, which is performed with a partner. I've had my forms unofficially assessed over the last couple of months with few corrections, if any, so I'm feeling confident.

Fingers crossed. I'll also try and get some video footage to upload. Wish me luck.
