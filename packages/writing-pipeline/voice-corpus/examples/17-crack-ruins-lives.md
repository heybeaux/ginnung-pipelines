# Crack ruins lives

During our warm-up this morning we were asked to stretch in a variety of positions. We started with wrists, then worked our way to elbows and shoulders and then up to the neck. The stretches were pretty generic. Look up. Look down. Look to the left. Look to the right. Rotate 3 times in each direction. Nothing complicated so far. We were then asked to look down and roll our heads slightly to the left. Done. We then had to put a little weight on the back of our heads to aid the stretch.

CRACK!

I felt a sudden pull in the back of my neck and left shoulder. It took my breath away and I could barely move. Ryan, the guy standing next to me, asked if it was my neck that made the cracking noise. It must have been louder than I thought.

I told my Sifu that I had pulled a muscle and I was in incredible pain. He said "Just focus on your kicks."

It looks like the only way to get out of training in China is to die.

Hmmm…
