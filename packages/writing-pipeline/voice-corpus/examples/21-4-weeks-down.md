# 4 weeks down

Tomorrow marks 4 weeks since I got on a plane in Brisbane to head to China and start this adventure. It has actually gone a lot quicker than I thought it would. In such a short time I have learnt so much about myself and have managed to empty about 90% of the useless thoughts from my head. I feel like I'm making progress, both in the martial arts and in my life. In 4 weeks I feel like I have grown. I know that might sound a little premature, but I honestly feel like I'm settling in to my own skin. I'm starting to feel like I know what I want, and that I know who I am. Maybe I've just been so intent on seeing and experiencing personal growth that I'm noticing it in the smallest details. Or maybe each and every bit of growth I'm experiencing is so long overdue that it's incredibly noticeable. Either way, I'm glad I decided to man up an get on the plane back in Brisbane. If I didn't, who knows where I would be right now?

Here's to hoping the next 4 weeks bring just as much growth and development. As difficult as it can be, I'm glad I'm changing.

Be like water.
